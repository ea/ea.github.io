Tenda Backdoor

Oct 18th, 2013

So, devttyS0 recently published his find of a backdoor in certain D-Link routers. Since there are a bunch of cheap chinese routers around here, and Tenda routers being especially cheap and popular, I wanted to take a look.

Long story short, devttyS0 beat me to it and published another awsome backdoor find. The backdoor is a bit more involved than the D-Link one. Awesome work!
